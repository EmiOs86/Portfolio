Hello!

Emi Osborn was born and raised in Beijing, China. She finished collage with an Industrial Design degree (also self studied interior design) and after that, she was lost. She spent a year to figure out what she really wanted, she even learnt Italian at one point because she thought getting into fashion buying in Italy is cool (Okay it still sounds cool!)

At the end she moved to Los Angeles to pursue her fashion design dream. She went to FIDM, after years of fashion design experiences, Emi started a doggie clothing brand “Le Petit Fluffy” in 2016. In early 2018, Emi has this idea to have a place full of joy to delight & inspire young women, and simplify their busy life. Le Petit Remedy was launched with this idea in mind.

For the past several years, Emi has been all over the place on her solopreneur journey. She tried so many thing: physical products, drop ship, print on demand, MLM etc. After years of trying, Emi finally realize she only interested in design branding for her new biz, build websites and that’s her super power! Most female solopreneurs are not tech savvy, where Emi’s super power comes in handy! Emi finally found her purpose after years: Help female solopreneurs start their side hustle with zero design & tech headache.
